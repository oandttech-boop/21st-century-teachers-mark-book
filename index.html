Installation & Setup Guide

System Requirements

  • Compatible Browsers: Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge.
  • Version: Ensure your browser is updated to the latest version.
  • Add-ons: Ensure extension/add-on usage is enabled in browser settings.
  • Network: A stable internet connection is required during installation.

Admin Configuration

  1. Log in to your environment (Google Sheets, LMS, or School Management System).
  2. Navigate to the Add-ons/Extensions section and confirm 21st Century Teacher's Markbook is enabled.
  3. Go to your Markbook Dashboard.
  4. for Mobile or Desktop users, + tab on the sheet can be used to add a new sheet to record for a particular learner.
  5. The Subjects you entered in the sheet 1(A7:A15) will be updated to Other sheets. .This reduces stress of entering subjects to individual sheets.
  6. Click the optional (reset ) button to reset your sheet if you have messed up your sheet .
  7. ClickRun Score Button to view results summary on sheet2 sheet3 and sheet4 .
  8. Click the "Next term" button to start a fresh for next term and your brought forward (B/F) for the learner will be updated. Please ensure that you export your data before clicking “Next term” .
  9. For Desktop users only, Click the menu bar at the right of your sheet to insert a new sheet and run your scores .

Step-by-Step Installation

1

Open a Google Sheet.

2

Go to Extensions > Add-ons > Get add-ons.

3

Search for "21st Century Teacher's Markbook".

4

Click Install and grant the necessary permissions.

Post-Installation Quick Tips

  • Manual Grading: Input grades directly into the designated data matrices.
  • Automated Dashboard: Check performance matrices, class averages, and diagnostic analytics instantly.
  • Troubleshooting: If the add-on fails to load, verify device storage limits and clear your browser cache.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new student to the Markbook?

Go to your Markbook Dashboard, select cell B2 on the active sheet, and input the student's name details.

How do I update a student's grade?

Simply change or enter the new data point into the score matrix. The system computations and overall grades will update dynamically . Then, click the analyse score button to view the results summary on sheet2 , sheet3 and sheet4.

Can I export student data to a CSV file?

Yes. Access the Markbook Dashboard interface, select the "3 dot on the right" choose share/export CSV formatting standard, and trigger the localized file download.

How do I view a student's performance report?

Navigate to the specific student profile sheets to view individual metrics, position ranks, and percentage curves. For system-wide diagnostics, view the initial dashboard sheet for macro-level subject metric analytics.
